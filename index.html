Vā is a Sāmoan concept.

The most famous definition was written by Sāmoan scholar Albert Wendt—‘Va is the space between, the betweenness, not empty space, not space that separates but space that relates, that holds separate entities and things together in the Unity-that-is-All, the space that is context, giving meaning to things.’
